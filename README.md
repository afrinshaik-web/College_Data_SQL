# College_Data_SQL

PROBLEM STATEMENT :

Create a College Management Database and analyze student performance, course enrollment, and faculty workload

APPROACH IN ANALYSING THE PROJECT :

PHASE 1: BASIC QUERIES

List all Computer Science students , Find courses with capacity greater than 50 , Show faculty with Professor designation , List all students who enrolled in the current semester , Show all courses offered by the Electronics department

PHASE 2: JOIN OPERATIONS

Student enrollments with course names and grades , Courses with faculty names and departments , All students with their enrollment count , List all faculty with the courses they teach and student enrollment count per course , Get each student's department along with their latest enrollment grade

PHASE 3: AGGREGATE QUERIES

Count students by department and gender , Average credits by department , Courses with high enrollment (> 80% capacity) , Find the course with the maximum number of students enrolled , Calculate the average number of courses taken per student by department

PHASE 4: ANALYSIS & INSIGHTS

Grade distribution by department , Faculty workload analysis , Department performance summary , Identify departments where average grades improved or declined across semesters , Show top 3 faculty by student enrollments handled, with course breakdown

PHASE 5: Summary of the insights and recommendations if any.
